== Description ==

[ReWeby](https://wordpress.org/plugins/reweby/) plugin, helps to record and replay the admin users and others web activity. It records the activity of the users in your site where they click the most, what they view the most in your site.


= Advanatages : =

* ReWeby records the admin users and nomral users click, mouse moves, and scroll activities on your site.
* Helps to monitor the site handlers doing any illegal activities on the site.
* Records the whole movements and activities that happens in website.
